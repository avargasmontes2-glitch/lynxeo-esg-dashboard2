Strategic Decarbonization Recommendations

1. Renewable Energy Transition (Scope 2)

Transition to 100% renewable electricity through PPAs and on-site solar installations. Priority focus on European production sites with highest energy consumption.

High Impact: -28,200 tCO₂e/year

2. Supplier Engagement Program (Scope 3 Upstream)

Implement supplier decarbonization requirements, focusing on top 20 copper and aluminum suppliers representing 65% of purchased goods emissions. Require SBTi targets alignment.

High Impact: -36,500 tCO₂e/year

3. Process Optimization & Electrification (Scope 1)

Replace natural gas heating systems with electric alternatives in extrusion and curing processes. Invest in heat recovery systems across manufacturing sites.

Medium Impact: -12,800 tCO₂e/year

4. Circular Economy - Material Innovation

Increase recycled content in cable production from current 15% to 40% by 2030. Develop take-back programs for end-of-life cables and implement closed-loop recycling.

High Impact: -18,600 tCO₂e/year
